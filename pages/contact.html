Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our development process, pricing structure, and project timelines.

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple web applications typically take 6-12 weeks, while complex enterprise solutions may require 6-12 months. We provide detailed timeline estimates during our initial consultation, including key milestones and delivery phases.

We offer both fixed-price and time-and-materials pricing models. Fixed-price works best for well-defined projects, while T&M provides flexibility for evolving requirements. All pricing includes detailed technical specifications, regular progress updates, and post-launch support. We provide transparent cost breakdowns during the proposal phase.

Our comprehensive process includes: requirements analysis, technical architecture design, agile development with regular demos, quality assurance testing, deployment and DevOps setup, documentation, training, and 90-day post-launch support. We maintain transparent communication throughout with weekly progress reports and milestone reviews.

We provide 90 days of complimentary support for bug fixes and minor adjustments. Beyond that, we offer flexible maintenance packages including security updates, performance monitoring, feature enhancements, and technical support. Many clients choose to continue working with us for ongoing development and system evolution.

Security is fundamental to our process. We sign NDAs before any detailed discussions, use secure development practices, implement comprehensive security testing, and maintain SOC 2 compliance. All code repositories are private, communications are encrypted, and we follow industry best practices for data protection and intellectual property security.
